Italian-meatballs

description

Now this is a delicacy that has been in the kersout family for decades. Some people know it as Italian meatballs. We just say ordinary meatballs.
But damn they are delicious.

Yes, this recipe is as simple as 123 so follow along and enjoy your delicious meal.

Ingedrients

  • 1/2 cup italian seasoned bread crumbs
  • 1/4 cup grated Romano cheese
  • 2 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 1/2 pounds ground beef

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C)
  2. Mix bread crumbs, Romano cheese, parsley, salt, pepper, garlic powder, and onion powder together.
    Combine with water and eggs. Add ground beef and mix well.
    Roll mixture into balls and place on a nonstick baking sheet.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until browned, about 30 minutes.
